## Family Tree Feature

### What is the Family Tree?
The Family Tree allows you to add information about your family members, creating a complete family profile that helps potential matches understand your family background.

### How to Use Family Tree

**Step 1: Access Your Profile**
1. Log in to your account
2. Click on "Edit Profile" from your dashboard
3. Scroll to the "Family Tree" section at the bottom

**Step 2: Add Family Members**
1. Click the "Add Family Member" button
2. Fill in the following details:
   - **Name** (Required) - Full name of the family member
   - **Age** - Current age
   - **Relationship** (Required) - Select from the dropdown
   - **Profession** - Their occupation
   - **Education** - Educational qualifications
   - **Gotra** - Gotra information if applicable

**Step 3: Manage Family Members**
- **Edit**: Click the edit icon to modify details
- **Delete**: Click the trash icon to remove a member
- **View**: Hover over a member to see all details

**Supported Relationships:**
- Father, Mother
- Paternal Grandfather, Paternal Grandmother
- Maternal Grandfather, Maternal Grandmother
- Brother, Sister

### Tips
- Add all family members for a complete profile
- Update information if any details change
- Your family tree helps matches understand your background
- Some members may choose not to view this information

## Referral & Earn Rewards Program

### What is the Referral Program?
Invite your friends to VipraPariwaar and earn rewards for each successful match! It's our way of thanking you for helping others find their perfect partner.

### How to Get Your Referral Code

**Step 1: Visit Referral Dashboard**
1. Log in to your account
2. Click on "Refer & Earn" from your dashboard
3. Or navigate to `/referral` directly

**Step 2: Copy Your Code**
1. Your unique referral code will display (format: VIPRA + 6 characters)
2. Click the copy button to copy the code
3. Share it with your friends

### How to Share Your Referral

#### Method 1: Direct Code Sharing
- Copy your referral code
- Share via WhatsApp, email, or any messaging app
- Format: "Join VipraPariwaar using code: VIPRA123456"

#### Method 2: Referral Link
1. Click "Share Link" button
2. Copy the complete referral link
3. Share via any platform

#### Method 3: Email Invite
1. Click "Email Friends"
2. Enter friend's email and name
3. We'll send them an invite with your code

#### Method 4: Social Media
1. Click "Share on Social"
2. Share directly to Twitter/Facebook/Instagram
3. Your code will be included

### How Rewards Work

**Referral Process:**
1. You share your referral code or link
2. Your friend signs up using your code
3. When your friend completes profile verification
4. You earn ₹500 reward
5. Reward appears in your "Pending Earnings"

**Earning Milestones:**
- First referral: ₹500
- Every successful referral: ₹500
- Bonus for 5 referrals: ₹2,500 total
- Bonus for 10 referrals: ₹5,000 total

### How to Check Your Referrals

**Dashboard Shows:**
- **Total Referrals**: All people you've invited
- **Successful Referrals**: Those who completed registration
- **Total Earnings**: All rewards earned (claimed + pending)
- **Pending Earnings**: Rewards not yet claimed

**Referral Status:**
- **Pending**: Friend hasn't completed registration yet
- **Completed**: Friend completed registration
- **Claimed**: You've claimed the reward

### How to Redeem Rewards

**To Claim Your Earnings:**
1. Go to Referral Dashboard
2. Check "Pending Earnings" amount
3. Click "Redeem ₹XXX Rewards"
4. Choose redemption method:
   - Bank Transfer
   - Wallet Credit
   - Gift Voucher
5. Confirm the transaction
6. Rewards processed within 5-7 business days

**Withdrawal Limits:**
- Minimum: ₹100
- Maximum: No limit
- Can withdraw anytime
- Processing fee: None (Free!)

### Tips for Maximum Referrals

1. **Share with Relevant People**: Friends, family, community members looking for matches
2. **Use Multiple Channels**: Combine different sharing methods for better reach
3. **Personal Message**: Add a personal note when sharing - it increases conversion
4. **Regular Sharing**: Keep sharing as you meet more people
5. **Follow Up**: Remind friends to complete their registration after signing up

### Referral FAQs

**Q: Can I share the same code multiple times?**  
A: Yes! Your referral code never expires. Share it with as many people as you want.

**Q: When do I get paid?**  
A: When your referred person completes profile verification.

**Q: Can I track who signed up from my code?**  
A: Yes, you can see all referrals and their status on the referral dashboard.

**Q: What if my friend doesn't complete registration?**  
A: The referral stays pending. Once they complete, you earn the reward.

**Q: Can I redeem partial amounts?**  
A: Yes, you can redeem any amount above ₹100.

**Q: Is there a limit on referrals?**  
A: No! Refer as many people as you want and earn unlimited rewards.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't See Family Tree?
- Check if you're logged in
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

### Referral Code Not Working?
- Ensure code is copied correctly
- Check for extra spaces
- Verify code hasn't expired
- Try resetting the code
- Contact support if issue persists

### Can't See Blog Posts?
- Check content is published
- Verify you have viewing permissions
- Try refreshing the page
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache

### Not Receiving Referral Rewards?
- Check referral status
- Ensure friend completed registration
- Wait 24 hours for processing
- Check spam folder for notifications
- Contact support with referral code
